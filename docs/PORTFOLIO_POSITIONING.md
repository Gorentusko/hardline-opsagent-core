# Portfolio Positioning

## One-liner

Hardline OpsAgent Core is a Docker-deployable AI operations assistant that turns system and container state into health reports, runbooks, and future tickets/docs.

## What to say in interviews

I built OpsAgent Core to show practical DevOps/platform engineering skills: containerized deployment, API design, observability thinking, AI provider abstraction, report generation, safe defaults, and documentation discipline.

## What not to claim

- Do not call it AGI.
- Do not claim it replaces monitoring platforms.
- Do not claim it performs autonomous remediation.
- Do not claim production readiness before v1.0.

## Strong claim

It is a working prototype of a safe, model-agnostic AI operations layer.
